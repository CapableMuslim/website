# Nutrition Fundamentals: Building a Foundation for Optimal Health

## Understanding Macronutrients

**Macronutrients** are the building blocks of nutrition: proteins, carbohydrates, and fats. Each plays a critical role in energy production, cellular repair, and metabolic health. Understanding how to balance these nutrients is essential for optimizing performance and longevity.

## The Role of Micronutrients

**Micronutrients**—vitamins and minerals—support immune function, cognitive performance, and overall well-being. Deficiencies can lead to fatigue, poor recovery, and chronic health issues.

## Building Your Nutrition Foundation

A sustainable nutrition plan prioritizes whole foods, adequate protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates. Consistency and personalization are key to long-term success.

## Actionable Steps

1. Track your food intake for one week to understand your baseline
2. Prioritize protein at every meal (0.8-1g per pound of body weight)
3. Choose whole, unprocessed foods whenever possible
4. Stay hydrated with at least 8 glasses of water daily
5. Adjust your nutrition based on your activity level and goals
